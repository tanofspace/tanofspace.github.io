Research Interests

Broadly speaking, I am interested in deepening my understanding of galaxy formation and evolution. My current research focuses on utilizing metal absorption lines to probe the CGM of simulated galaxies, as well as the IGM in cosmological simulations. I also investigate the evolution of these metal absorption lines from redshift 5 to the present, potentially illuminating the evolution of metals and/or ions across cosmic time.

    Here is a list of keywords related to my research interests:
  • the circumgalactic medium (CGM)
  • absorption line studies (especially metals)
  • galaxy formation and evolution
  • galaxy dynamics and kinematics
  • machine learning applications in astronomy
  • statistical inference from large astronomical datasets
